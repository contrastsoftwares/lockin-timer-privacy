Privacy Policy

Lock In Timer — Focus Session App

Last updated: February 10, 2025

Lock In Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully respected.

Overview

Lock In Timer is a focus session timer app designed to help you stay productive. The app operates entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to function.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage data or analytics are collected
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

Data Storage

Any timer settings or preferences you configure are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Lock In Timer does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Lock In Timer is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Screen pinning / Lock Task mode: Used to keep the timer in the foreground during a focus session, helping you stay focused by preventing accidental navigation away from the app.

No permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
